What happens to my studyhalls when I share?

Sharing a studyhall allows your classmates to see what you’ve been studying with your buddies. It’s a great way to collaborate even after your study sesh is over, and it lets your buddies get involved even if they were busy at the time.

What happens to my notebooks when I share?

Sharing notebooks allows your classmates to see what subjects you’re working on in the course activity feed. It doesn’t share your private notes, though, so you can still keep secrets.

What happens to my notes when I share?

Sharing notes allows your classmates to read notes that you take. So share ‘em and maybe that cutie from lecture will set up a studyhall with you. Plus, you can always make them private if kids start to mooch.

How do I find buddies?

The best ways to find friends on Studyhall.com are:

  1. Add courses to your profile. Then see who is in your classes to pick Study Buddies.
  2. Invite your friends from Facebook or GMail.
  3. Use the Search feature to find your friends by name or email address
