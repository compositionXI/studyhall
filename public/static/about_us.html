Our core mission is to make studying social. We’re here to make your life as a student easier… and more fun. StudyHall helps you improve your grades, meet new classmates, and study socially.

StudyHall’s core team is a group of rockstar, educational gurus.

StudyHall’s Team

Ross Blankenship
Founder & CEO
Cornell, Wash U Law

Ross is the Founder and CEO of StudyHall. Ross graduated from the NMH School, Cornell University and Washington University School of Law. Ross has a tremendous amount of experience in higher education, including founding a company called Top Test Prep, which has since become a major force in the test prep industry.
